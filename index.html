Our Story

Dave Wierer founded the shop in 1983. His daughter Jessica runs it now.

Dave Wierer opened Dave's Auto Body Inc at 1748 West Paulson Road in Green Bay in 1983. He worked that bay for 35 years. In 2018, his daughter Jessica Senn took over as owner and president, and the shop kept its name, its address, and the same crew on the floor.

Forty-two years later, the shop is still on Paulson Road. The phone numbers still route to the same building. Most of the technicians on the floor have been there longer than most national chains have had a Green Bay location. That continuity is the reason a customer who had a fender repaired in 2008 can bring a teenager's first car back in 2026 and recognize the names on the work order.

"The same address since 1983. The same family. A daughter-led second generation, and a crew with roughly 89 combined years on the bay."

The shop runs collision repair, refinishing, towing, and used-vehicle sales out of one Paulson Road building. The tagline on the wall reads "honesty is the best way to gain the trust of customers." That is the voice the family chose, and it is the voice the site is built around.
